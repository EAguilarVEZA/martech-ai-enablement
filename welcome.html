The part that matters most

You're not being replaced. You're getting time back.

Let's say it plainly: this is not about doing more with fewer people. Claude is a tool — think of it as a fast, tireless assistant for the slow parts of the job. The drafting, the summarizing, the formatting, the first pass at a spreadsheet or a deck: the things that used to eat an afternoon can take minutes once you know how to ask.

What stays yours is the part no tool can do — your judgment, your relationships, your creativity, your understanding of our work. That's exactly where we want your time and energy to go. Claude clears the busywork; you do the thinking.
