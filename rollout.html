Extreme Ownership

Rollout plan, certification & adoption

A guide only works if people actually use it. Here is a simple cadence to run the program, a way to recognize people who finish, and what to measure so you know it is working.

How people learn

70 / 20 / 10

Roughly 70% from doing real work, 20% from peers, 10% from formal training. So weight everything toward this week's real tasks, make it easy to share wins, and keep formal lessons short.

Do-along

Never just watch

Every session ends with people in Claude using their own input. The hands-on lessons and the coach already do this — protect that.

Stay unblocked

Office hours + coach

A weekly 30-minute office hour plus the always-on University Coach catches people before they give up. A shared Teams/Slack channel for prompts and wins does the rest.

Measure adoption

Know it's working

Track the things that show real change, not vanity metrics:

  • Which workflows people actually moved to Claude
  • Lessons completed and Challenge entries submitted
  • Time saved on a few named tasks (ask people)
  • Prompts and wins shared in the team channel

Surface the best stories — nothing drives adoption like a peer's real win.
