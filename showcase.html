See it in action: the 5-minute wow

The fastest way to make Claude click for a room is to watch it go from messy notes to a polished deliverable in one sitting. Run these four steps live in a kickoff — each builds on the last.

Step 1 · Notes to brief

Turn messy notes into a campaign brief

Prompt
Turn these rough notes into a campaign brief: objective, audience, insight, core message, offer, channels, assets, timeline, owners, risks, approval checklist. Ask me anything important that's missing first.

Notes:
[paste real, non-sensitive notes]
Step 2 · Brief to deck

Turn the brief into a deck outline

Prompt
Using the brief you just wrote, create a 10-slide leadership deck outline. Per slide: a takeaway headline, 2-3 bullets, a suggested visual, and a speaker note.
Step 3 · Deck to mockup

Turn a key slide into an on-brand mockup

Open Claude Design and paste this:

Prompt
Design a one-page landing mockup for the campaign in this brief: hero with headline and subhead, 3 benefit blocks, a primary CTA, and a footer. Keep it clean and on-brand.
Step 4 · Polish & verify

Sharpen it and check it

Prompt
Improve the brief for a busy executive: clearer, more concise, specific about impact. Then list any claims or numbers I should verify before sharing.

That's the wow: messy notes to a brief, a deck, a mockup, and a verified version — in about five minutes. See Verify your output for the checking habit.
