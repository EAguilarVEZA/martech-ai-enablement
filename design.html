Claude Design

Claude Design is Anthropic's design canvas: describe what you want in chat and Claude builds a polished, on-brand design on the canvas beside it. Great for fast campaign mockups, slide decks, one-pagers, and prototypes the martech team can ship or hand off.

What it is

Chat on the left, canvas on the right

Tell Claude what you need and it generates a working design you can refine live. No blank artboard, no manual pixel-pushing to get a strong first draft.

On brand

Use your design system

Import design systems from a GitHub repo, design files, or uploaded assets. Claude builds with your approved components and checks its output against them before showing you.

Refine

Edit inline, apply everywhere

Comment on a specific element, edit text directly, or use adjustment knobs for spacing, color, and layout, then ask Claude to apply a change across the whole design.

What you can make

Practical martech assets

  • Campaign landing page and email mockups
  • Leadership and campaign-update slide decks
  • One-pagers, briefs, and sell sheets
  • Clickable prototypes to test a flow
  • On-brand social and ad concepts
Share & export

Get it out the door

  • Share an internal link, or give edit access to collaborate live

  • Hand a build-ready bundle to Claude Code to ship the real thing

Starter prompt

Your first design to try

Design a one-page campaign landing mockup for [offer] aimed at [audience].

Include: a hero with headline and subhead, 3 benefit blocks, a primary CTA, a short FAQ, and a footer.

Keep it clean and on-brand with a [your-brand] accent color. Then suggest 2 headline variations I can A/B test.
Paste this into Claude Design to generate your first canvas.
Guardrail

Responsible use

Don't paste sensitive patient or confidential data into design prompts. Keep a human review before anything goes external, and route brand or legal-sensitive work for approval.
