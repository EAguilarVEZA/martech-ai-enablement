Claude account types

Which Claude plan fits which person. Pricing changes, so the live numbers live on Anthropic's pricing page — this is the plain-language difference between them.

Free

Try Claude

Explore Claude on web, desktop, and mobile with limited usage. Good for a first look before committing.

Best for: curious first-timers.

Pro

Individual regular use

About 5× the free usage per session, priority access during busy times, early access to new features, and Claude Code in the terminal — all in one subscription. Around $20/month.

Best for: someone using Claude most days.

Max

Power users

Higher limits than Pro, in two tiers: Max 5× and Max 20× the Pro usage per session, plus priority access to the newest models and features.

Best for: heavy daily users who hit Pro limits.

Team

Teams & shared admin

Up to 150 seats with central billing, single sign-on, domain capture, just-in-time provisioning, and role-based permissions. Standard seats get a bit more usage than Pro.

Best for: a department that wants one managed workspace.

Enterprise

Large, regulated orgs

Everything in Team plus advanced security and compliance controls and more scale. The seat fee covers access; usage is billed separately at API rates.

Best for: orgs with strict security and compliance needs.

For our team

What I'd pick

  • Most of the team: a Team workspace — one place to manage seats, SSO, and billing.
  • Power users who hit limits: add Max for them.
  • Strict security or compliance: Enterprise.
  • Just exploring: Pro for an individual.
Note

Two other things to know

  • Developers who build on Claude use the API, billed by usage — separate from these chat plans.
  • Pricing and exact limits change. Always confirm current numbers on the official pricing page before deciding.
